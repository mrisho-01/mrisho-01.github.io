👤 Career Profile

Dental researcher and orthodontist with expertise in CBCT analysis of alveolar bone defects and machine learning applications for predicting bone fenestration/dehiscence. Currently pursuing a Master's in Dentistry (Orthodontics) at Jinzhou Medical University, China.
